## Sustainability

### "How do you keep it running?"

Good question! Here's our approach:

1. **Efficient Architecture**
   - Serverless functions (only pay for usage)
   - Static assets (cached globally)
   - Minimal database queries
   - Optimized images

2. **Free Tier Limits**
   - MongoDB: 512MB (thousands of stories)
   - Vercel: 100GB bandwidth/month
